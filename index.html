About Me

Dan is an experienced Manual Quality Assurance Analyst, skilled in analyzing functional, user experience, and technical requirements during the complete software life cycle. Has strong attention to detail ensuring issues are logged and resolved prior to launch. Experienced critical thinker who adapts quickly to changing environments.

Dan has Ten plus years as a Quality Assurance Functional Testing Analyst. Works as a Product Owner and Business Process Analyst in an Agile environment. Serves as a SME in Design, Development, Test Integration, Bug Tracking and UAT Testing to support current Software being developed, by creating User Stories and interviewing the Product End User to ensure all acceptance criteria has been met. Creates Test Cases based off of verified User Stories. Handles release planning, Triage meetings and requirement reviews. Worked with Offshore Teams to manage User Stories, Defects and Test Cases.
